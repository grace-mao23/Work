Foundation: Where Greatness Starts

Purple Pancakes Presents the Average Person's Guide to Foundation

Foundation is a basic front-end framework tool to get anyone started with their own webpage.

Here are the key features we'd like to present you with:

Cells
Cells are exactly as they sound. They separate text.

Grid
Grids are also exactly as they sound. They separate into boxes.

Form
Forms are yet again exactly as they sound. They allow the users to fill things in.
